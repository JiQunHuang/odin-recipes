Egg Fried Rice Recipe

Description

Taken from christieathome

10 minute Egg Fried Rice. Delicious, simple fried rice seasoned with soy sauce and sesame oil with eggs and green onions. This only requires 6 ingredients!

Ingredients

  • 2 cups cooked jasmine rice at least 1 day old
  • 1 stalk green onion finely chopped
  • 2.5 tbsp regular soy sauce not light or dark!
  • Â½ tbsp sesame oil
  • 4 eggs whisked
  • 2 tbsp avocado oil or vegetable oil

Instructions

  1. Over medium high heat in a steel wok, add 1 tbsp of cooking oil and allow it to smoke. Important: if using a non-stick pan, do NOT smoke oil and keep it on medium heat allowing the oil to get hot.
  2. Once oil begins to smoke, pour in whisked eggs and quickly stir to form shape. Once the eggs form shape but are still moist, quickly toss and mix in rice. If rice is clumped together, break apart in pan.
  3. Sweep everything to the side, add remaining oil into empty space with green onions. Quickly mix green onions into rice and eggs.
  4. Add soy sauce and sesame oil. Mix until grains are coloured brown and immediately remove off heat. Serve hot and enjoy!
